Club Tracker Privacy Policy

Club Tracker collects the following data elements and for the given reasons only.
Name: Given, Middle, Surname, Suffix and Title
To help identify the correct records and authorizations for each person.
In the case of a medical emergency where a parent or emergency contact is not available, to be able to provide appropriate identification to medical personnel.
Friendly Name
To help identify the correct records for each person.
Gender
To help place clubbers in appropriate groups.
Full Address
For mailings of announcements and birthday cards
Age and Birth date
For sending appropriate birthday cards.
Age Group
The typical school class a clubber belongs to.
Used to assign the appropriate club and curriculum for each clubber.
Email
Used to send invitations and connect your Social Auth login account with your records, rights, and privileges in the system.
May be used by club or program leaders to send appropriate communication.
Media Disclosure Agreement
Optionally used by clubs if they desire to provide pictures of clubbers on the internet.
Phone
Optionally collected for contact in a medical emergency.
Known Allergies/Medical Conditions
So that allergies can be avoided if any snacks are provided.
Emergency Contact
Provided in case a parent cannot be contacted in a medical emergency.
Preferred Doctor
May be used in an medical emergency to guide selection of a provider if possible and in the absence of a parent or emergency contact.

If you would like to change or remove specific data elements, you can do so from your registration screen.

To have your family's information completely deleted, contact the site owner and make a request from the email address of an adult who has logged into the site that you want deleted.
